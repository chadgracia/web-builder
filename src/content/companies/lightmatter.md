For those seeking access to Lightmatter, one of the leading private companies in AI data center infrastructure and a partner in NVIDIA's chip-connection ecosystem, we have an SPV in the current round at 0/0 — no management fee, no carried interest.

Lightmatter builds hardware that moves data between chips using light instead of copper wire. As AI clusters have grown, the constraint has shifted from raw computing power to the cost and power draw of connecting the chips together. Lightmatter builds the full system rather than a single part, and its technology is designed into the chip itself, which makes it difficult to swap out once adopted. Its products moved into customer testing this year with three of the major chip manufacturing and packaging firms.
